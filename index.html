About MoodMusic

MoodMusic is a passion project built to help you discover music that resonates with your emotions. We believe music is the universal language of feelings.

Simply type how you're feeling — happy, sad, energetic, heartbroken, or anything in between — and we'll curate a playlist of real songs from multiple languages that match your mood.

Our carefully handpicked catalog spans English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Spanish, Korean, Japanese, and Punjabi — because great music has no language barrier. 🎶
